Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026  ·  Best In Bhubaneswar

This Cookie Policy explains how Best In Bhubaneswar ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website at bestinbhubaneswar.in. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, work more efficiently, and to provide information to website owners. Cookies do not give us access to your device or any information about you other than the data you choose to share with us.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage — these work in a comparable way to cookies and are covered by this policy.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To make our website work: Some cookies are essential for the site to function correctly. Without these, parts of the site would not work properly.
  • To remember your preferences: We use cookies to remember your cookie consent choice so we don't ask you again on every page.
  • To understand how visitors use our site: We use analytics cookies to understand which pages are most popular, how visitors move around the site, and to improve the user experience.
  • To show relevant advertising: We use Google AdSense and other advertising partners who may set cookies to show you relevant advertisements based on your interests and browsing behaviour.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Required for the website to function. Cannot be switched off. Cookie consent preference, session management Session / 1 year

4. Third-Party Cookies

We work with third-party services that may also set cookies on your device. These include:

  • Google AdSense — Used to display personalised advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Google Analytics — Used to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site. We use this to improve our content and user experience.
  • YouTube — When we embed videos from YouTube, YouTube may set cookies on your device. These are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective third-party privacy policies for more information.

5. Google AdSense and Personalised Ads

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense uses cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your interests and previous visits to this and other websites. You have the following options to control this:

  • Visit the Google Ads Settings page to opt out of personalised advertising.
  • Visit www.aboutads.info/choices to opt out of personalised advertising from participating companies.
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent data collection by Google Analytics.

Even if you opt out of personalised ads, you will still see advertisements — they just will not be tailored to your interests.

6. How to Manage and Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or decline cookies (other than strictly necessary ones). You can exercise your cookie preferences using our cookie consent banner, which appears when you first visit our site.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from particular sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that if you choose to block or delete cookies, some features of the website may not function correctly. Browser-specific guidance:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

7. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website after being shown our cookie consent banner, and by clicking "Accept All", you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. If you click "Decline", we will only use strictly necessary cookies required for the site to function.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies and refreshing the page, which will trigger the cookie banner to appear again.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our practices. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
