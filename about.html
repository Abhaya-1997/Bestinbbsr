Who We Are

Bhubaneswar's Most Practical City Guide

Straight-talking, street-level information for everyone who calls the Temple City home — from first-time movers to long-time residents.

Best In Bhubaneswar was built out of frustration. After spending years trying to piece together reliable information about everyday life in this city — from fair auto fares to honest school fees — we decided to stop waiting and build the resource ourselves.

Bhubaneswar is one of India's fastest-growing cities, yet most of the information available online about living here is either tourist-focused, outdated, or copied from national aggregators that have never actually set foot in Khandagiri or Saheed Nagar. We are here to change that.

What We Cover

Our focus is purely practical: the things you need to know before you sign a lease, enrol your child in school, set up your internet connection, or figure out how to navigate Mo Bus on a Monday morning. We cover the real Bhubaneswar — not the one in the tourism brochures.

The Person Behind This Site

Rajan Pattnaik
Founder · Digital Marketer · Bhubaneswar Resident

I am a digital marketing professional who has been living and working in Bhubaneswar for several years. I built Best In Bhubaneswar because I got tired of giving new arrivals the same answer — "just ask around." People deserve something better than word-of-mouth when they are trying to manage a budget, find a good mechanic, or understand their electricity bill.

Everything on this site is based on direct fieldwork, personal experience, and conversations with people who actually live and work here. No paid placements. No fluff.

How We Verify Our Information

Every price guide on this site is based on direct visits to local markets, calls to service providers, and cross-checks with residents in the relevant area. We note the date each guide was last updated and revisit high-traffic pages regularly to keep figures current. When we are uncertain, we say so — and we always encourage you to confirm prices directly before making a decision.
